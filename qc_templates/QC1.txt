FOOTER:
All hubs are left in place after excavation of footing
Footing spoils are removed or spread in place for future access
Rebar installed as specified with min overlap and tie wire as needed
Sleeves for MEP/ Concrete
Check elevation according to specs and plans
Cylinder testing for the pour
All overpours to be cleaned up and disposed of by contractor
BLOCK FOUNDATION/STEM WALLS:
Check all dimensions - no more than 1/4" tolerance
Check placement of wall on footer - min 2" showing on each side
Measure spacing of anchors per specifications.
All anchors are free of mortar, straight and have a nut and washer
Ensure all waterproofing is installed in correct locations
Locate and mark all brick ledges and ensure min of 3" of bearing
Mortar has been cleaned and free of excess mortar
Beam pockets installed and checked for sizes and locations
UNDERGROUND PLUMBING:
Check sleeve locations, elevations & dimensions are per plan
Check dimensions from leading wall on all plumbing
- Stacks
- Vents
- Toilets
- Tub and shower drains
Check that main water is installed, staked and clearly marked
Cleanouts clearly marked and caps securely installed
Air test is maintained through slab prep
Excess granular fill is cleaned up and moved to next location
UNDERGROUND ELECTRIC:
All conduit installed per specifications and plans
All sweeps are bedded and securely fastened
Check dimensions from leading wall on all electric
- Island locations
SLAB PREP:
Foundation insulation installed and checked for R-value and locations
Pad is dry, frost free prior to placement of aggregate
Expansion joints identified and installed
Check thickened slab location, size and reinforcing
Ensure vapor barrier is installed with proper overlap
Tape vapor barrier around all penetrations
Ensure min 4" of aggregate installed 4" from finished floor elevation
Required reinforcing in slab is in place and secured as per specifications
Aggregate is dry, frost free prior to concrete placement
Excess granular fill is cleaned up and moved to next location
SLAB POUR:
Cylinders collected and returned to testing lab
Check FF & FL requirements (1/4" per 10', if applicable)
Slab poured level and free of visual low spots (FF & FL Requirements me
All penetrations to receive smooth troweled finish
Overpours are cleaned up and all debris removed
